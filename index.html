AURAVYBE

Turn play into power: Master AI Vibe Coding, own the game studio, and launch real startups as future-ready tech entrepreneurs.

Concept & Mission 🚀

  • The VYBE of AI Coding: We completely eliminate boring, repetitive code syntax homework drills. Instead, students dive straight into "Vibe Coding"—the advanced practice of directing top-tier autonomous software agents using natural language.
  • The AURA of Entrepreneurship: Every single creative development milestone is framed inside a realistic business scenario. Students learn to handle cloud infrastructure budgets, track live user metric arrays, and design real monetization platforms.
  • Network & Collaborative Pools: Both Middle and High School students are fully eligible to join. Parents are welcome and highly encouraged to share these technical labs with local friends to unlock additional cohort spaces!
